Instructions

You were minding you own business in Lilliput Steps and a wild Mole Playing Rough appeared to attack you!

Now you've got to use your guts (and your baseball bat) to shoo this Mole Playing Rough off.

Move using and swing the bat with the space bar.

You can bash the mole repeatedly, but you'll lose health if you accidentaly miss it.
